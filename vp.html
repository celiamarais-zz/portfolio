The Vendor Portal

Some of the challenges of an experience marketplace are sourcing and onboarding high quality, trustworthy providers, while managing a complex fulfillment model. When IfOnly first started, these tasks were done entirely manually and internally, at a very high cost.
The vendor portal's launch allowed us to start moving from a completely manual to a mostly automated supply acquisition and management process, a critical step toward scaling our business.
A number of tools let our providers create their own shop and products. Once fully vetted, they can fulfill, manage, and schedule their orders and experiences without any intervention from IfOnly.
The Interstitial Page is a new vendor's home base. From there, they can complete their profile, set up their payout information and start creating their products.
The Product Builder lets user create their listings. With many different possible product types, the goal was to simplify and streamline a very complex decision tree.
In their To-do List, vendors get an overview of their pending tasks. They can manage shipping, and schedule or make changes experiences.
The Conversations Module lets vendors and customers communicate without exchanging actual contact information, preserving both parties' privacy. It also allows us to control for quality, assuring the excellence of our customer service, a crucial part of IfOnly's brand.
The Dashboard gives vendors an overview of their shop's performance and statistics, and displays the day's most important tasks and messages.
Since its launch, the vendor portal has allowed us to vastly reduce our acquisition cost. It is constantly being improved, and features planned include improved scheduling, new buying modalities, and text messaging integration.
