MoCo Loco Rebranding and Redesign

Working with Tavish at Notice Bureau, we were asked to create a new identity and design for the popular modern design blog MoCo Loco.
Since MoCo focuses on industrial design and architecture, we took building blocks as the inspiration for the site design, and created different color systems for the various sub-sections of the blog. We also added a like system that allowed users to see how popular each item was at a glance.
For the rebranding, we used close-ups of textures and a fun, 3D rendering of MoCo's logo, as a wink to the blog's roots in the very material world of architecture and industrial design.
This rebranding and redesign lead to a significant increase in readership and revenue, and expanded MoCo's user base on tablets.
