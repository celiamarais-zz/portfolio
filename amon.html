Field Excursion

An immersive online experience created for the electronic musician Amon Tobin.
This experimental site, created for the launch of Amon's album, could only be navigated by moving your cursor. It was populated with a collection of strange, eerie creatures.
Reluctant at first, the gentle animals hid in the dark corners of the site, but with proper stimulation they opened up, and eagerly showed you their crazy dance moves.
Special attention was applied to the sound environment, with sounds panning and fading according to your position relative to creatures.
The site was named FWA of the day upon its launch, and the average visit time was counted in the minutes. It contributed to the visibility of Amon's shift from a "techno" sound (made mainly from samples and synthesized sounds), to a more organic one, with the new tracks based uniquely on real-world recordings.
