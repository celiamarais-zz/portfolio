Modular Buying Tools

IfOnly is an innovative experience marketplace that offers over 17 product types, all with very different characteristics. Most are real-world experiences, needing to be scheduled or personalized. Some can be auctioned, others have a tilt threshold...





We wanted to simplify our user experience, and allow our customers and vendors to negotiate the details of any experience at any time in the order cycle. So we created a flexible and modular system of discrete buy boxes.






This buy box system is responsive, and can be included with all its functionality on any page.






It offers a consistent experience to our users, while greatly simplifying development.




The vendor or customer makes a new request, or asks for a change in the existing experience. The other party agrees or declines the request, and a notification is sent out. The details of the experience are updated, and displayed within the same element.



The exchanges between vendors and customers happen without any intervention from IfOnly, a huge step toward scalability. And in the future, it will allow us to grow our business by letting partners and vendors include it on their own sites.
